Khil's life at a glace

  • Born: September 4, 1934, Smolensk, Russia
  • A Soviet-Russian baritone singer
  • A recipient of the People's Artist of the RSFSR
  • An Internet meme, often referred to as "Trololo" or "Russian Rickroll"
  • Spouse: Zoya Pravdina
  • Children: Dmitri Khil
  • Died: June 4, 2012, Saint Petersburg, Russia
